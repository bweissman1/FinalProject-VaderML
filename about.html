We put these accusations to the test and developed a Python program that scrapes the web for news articles involving President Donald Trump from several discrete time periods, performed a sentiment analysis on them, and graphed the mean sentiment on three axes: positive, negative, and neutral. 

We then sorted and visualized the data by media outlet, calculated the centroid of each one, and applied a k-nearest neighbors algorithm. Using this, our goal is to classify an article from an unknown news source into its correct place of origin.
