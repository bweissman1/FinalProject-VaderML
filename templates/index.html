Methodology


HOW IT'S DONE

From NLTK, a Vader Sentiment analysis is conducted on the article, delivering four points of measurement: negative, postive, neutral, and compound values

Using Matplotlib, the article is plotted and compared against 300 points of data from 10 different mainstream sources from outlets like CNN, WashPo, and NYTimes

The news article is scraped using Newspaper3k's article.text, delivering the article free of links, images, and other fluff
